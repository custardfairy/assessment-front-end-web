Intellectual Nomad

Greetings! I'm Holly: a magpie collector of puns, data, philosophy, and shiny art. I grew up in the deepest coal fields of Appalachia but have called Michigan my home since 2005. I attended Marshall University in West Virginia to earn a degree in English Education with a secondary emphasis in World History. Degree aside, my career began in online customer service and transitioned into support and data analytics.

My long and random list of interests include:

  • Board Games
  • Political Activism & Intersectional Feminism
  • Genre Fiction
  • Cooking
  • Gardening
  • Mixed Media Art
  • Playing Instruments
  • Singing with Others

Career Goals

As someone fascinated by data, I'm compelled to follow a career path that has data at its center. Similarly, I enjoy writing useful and clean code that automates dreary tasks or helps remove challenges from someone's day. While providing technical support for customers in the data security sphere, I became interested in the backend workings of Javascript and Python and set out to learn both. I'm excited to find that coding is similar to writing poetry - yet another interest of mine.
